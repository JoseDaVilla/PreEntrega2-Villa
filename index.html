Our Baggages Policies

We charge for carry-ons and checked luggage in order to maintain affordable ticket costs for everyone. For checked luggage weighing over 40 pounds, we charge extra. This is because the extra weight makes the plane burn through more gas. Your ticket includes one personal item that completely fits in the smaller sizer box (such as a purse, small backpack, etc.). Any additional personal items or larger items, such as a typical carry-on or checked bag, will require payment.
